# unboring: FAQs

| Question | Answer |
| --- | --- |
| is this for friends or dating? | friends, always. unboring is a friendship platform, not a dating one. it's currently for women, & it's about meeting people you'd genuinely want in your life. what grows from there is yours. |
| will there be people my age? | yes. everyone in your group is within a few years of your age & at a similar life stage, so you're meeting people you can actually relate to. |
| how does matching work? | when you join, you take a quick personality test. it places you in one of our five archetypes, & we use those to build balanced groups so every table has the right mix of personalities & the dynamic just works. at the end of each experience, we ask who you'd like to see again. if it's mutual, we set you up on your next experience together. |
| how many people am i meeting? | the first portion, usually an intimate shared meal, is with 5 to 7 others in your group. after that, groups from across the city come together for a bigger experience, anywhere from 20 to 100 members. |
| when will my reservation be revealed? | you'll receive your exact arrival time & location the day before your experience. |
| when do i find out about the second location? | about an hour into the first portion, we'll reveal where you're heading next. half the fun is not knowing until you're already in it. |
| what about dietary restrictions? | we ask about dietary needs during onboarding & do our best to accommodate them when we curate your night. |
| what does membership include? | we curate your entire night, from your reservation to your group to the experience that follows. food & drinks are down to you (unless stated otherwise). |
| how do i stay connected after? | every group gets a chat to keep things going, & the more you show up, host, & follow up with the people you've met, the more connection points & streaks you earn. it rewards the things that actually turn a night out into a friendship. |
