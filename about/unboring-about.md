# unboring: about section

**Label (small):** what is unboring?

**Hook (large standout line):**
> "we should do this!"

**Body:**

you've said it in the group chat a hundred times. then weeks go by and it never happens.

you're not lazy. making plans is hard, and following through is harder. so most of us stay in and scroll instead.

unboring fixes that. we help women make real friends in real life. we pick the place, sort the details and bring you together with women you'd love to meet. all you do is say yes and show up.

it's a simple way to get out, meet new people and grow your circle. no swiping, no DMs, no doom scrolling. just real nights out with real people.
